# SPC Chart App – Product & Engineering Plan

## Overview

This application allows users to:
- Upload or enter time-series data
- Generate SPC (Statistical Process Control) or Run Charts
- Receive automated statistical analysis and plain-English insights
- Save, revisit, and collaborate on datasets
- Apply custom visual themes
- Generate automated reports and presentations
- Share templates across teams

The goal is to move beyond “chart creation” into a collaborative decision-support and quality improvement platform, particularly for healthcare and operational improvement.

---

# Core Principles

- Prioritise clarity over statistical complexity
- Provide actionable insights, not just visuals
- Follow NHS “Making Data Count” guidance
- Design for non-statisticians
- Support collaboration across teams
- Keep exports presentation-ready
- Ensure accessibility and readability
